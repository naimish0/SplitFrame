SplitFrame helps users create photo collages, resize images, and merge videos. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

## Information the App Uses

SplitFrame lets you select photos and videos from your device so you can edit and export them. Selected media is used to show previews, apply edits, and create exported image or video files.

SplitFrame does not require an account and does not upload your selected photos or videos to a developer-owned server for editing.

## Files and Device Storage

The app may save exported images and videos to your device gallery or media storage when you choose to export. Temporary processing files may be created on your device and removed after export.

## Advertising

SplitFrame includes advertising. Advertising partners, including Google AdMob, may collect or receive information such as device identifiers, app interactions, diagnostics, and approximate ad-related data to provide, measure, and improve ads. Their collection and sharing practices are governed by their own policies.

Google privacy information:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob information:

https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

## Permissions

Depending on your Android version and selected actions, SplitFrame may request access needed to select media, save exported files, show export notifications, or run an export in the background.

## Data Sharing

SplitFrame does not sell your selected photos or videos. Data may be processed by third-party SDKs used in the app, such as advertising SDKs, according to their policies and your device settings.

## Children

SplitFrame is not designed for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us so we can review the request.

## Changes

This policy may be updated when the app changes. The updated version will be posted at the same privacy policy URL with a revised effective date.
